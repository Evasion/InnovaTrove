SMS Messaging Terms

Last updated: April 2025

About This SMS Program

InnovaTrove ("we", "us", "our") operates an SMS messaging program to communicate with customers about their service bookings. By providing your phone number and checking the consent box on your booking page, you agree to receive transactional and informational text messages related to your booking.

Types of Messages You May Receive

  • 1 Booking link — A secure link to review your service agreement and pay your deposit.
  • 2 Completion notice — A message notifying you that your service is complete and requesting your confirmation.
  • 3 Payment reminder — Final balance and Zelle payment details upon service confirmation.

How We Collect Consent

Your phone number is provided directly to InnovaTrove during the booking process (by you or by the business arranging the service on your behalf). Before any additional messages are sent, you are shown an explicit consent notice on your booking page:

"I agree to receive SMS text messages from InnovaTrove about my booking — including reminders and payment updates. Message frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time."

You must actively check this box before proceeding. Your consent, timestamp, and IP address are recorded in our system.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your booking activity. You will typically receive 2–4 messages per booking (initial link, completion notice, and final payment details). We do not send promotional or marketing messages.

How to Opt Out

You can opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message we send. After opting out, you will receive a one-time confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.

To re-enable messages, reply START. For help, reply HELP or contact us directly (see below).

Message & Data Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan. InnovaTrove does not charge for SMS messages. Contact your carrier for details about your plan.

Privacy

Your phone number is used solely to send you messages related to your booking. We do not sell, share, or use your number for any other purpose. No mobile information will be shared with third parties for marketing purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Contact Us

For questions about this SMS program, contact InnovaTrove at payments@innovatrove.com.
